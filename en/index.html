Hi, we are Higia

We are developing EVA, an accessible and non-invasive wearable device for breast cancer risk assessment.

EVA provides women with personalized and actionable information for early breast cancer assessment. Early breast cancer detection substantially increases the probability of successful treatments.

Female Founders

Rocío Paulina Martínez

Female Founders is our internal program for testing and get feedback on EVA. Thanks to the participation of voluntary women, we can continue to improve the development of EVA and compile data to train our artificial intelligence algorithms.

Learn about the history of Rocío, volunteer for our campaign.
