Our
Vision

We are empowering people to take control of their well-being through the development of innovative technology that allows the access to actionable information about their health.

Our history

2012

Graciela Ríos (mother of Julián Ríos) is diagnosed with breast cancer.

2016
January

The first idea of EVA is born "Professionalization of the self-exploration method of the breast".

May

Higia wins the Babson University Innovation Challenge with the concept of EVA.

June

Julian joins forces with Antonio Torres and José Lavariega. The formal development of EVA begins.

July

The first "minimum viable product" of EVA is born.

Higia hires its first collaborator: Fernando López (Director of software).

November

Higia wins the National Student Entrepreneur Award.

InvestoVC and its union of angel investors make the first external investment in Higia.

2017
January

Raymundo González (Director of Artificial Intelligence) and Martín Urtiz (Leading algorithm developer) join.

February

Higia gets first place at the Everis Awards (1 million MXN).

May

The investment fund ID345 invests in the pre-seed round of investment of Higia.

Higia becomes the first Mexican company to be awarded the Global Student Entrepreneur Award.

June

The Mexican President Enrique Peña Nieto awards Higia Tech with the Presidential Medal in Technology and Entrepreneurship.

July

EVA is presented at the Congress of Future Medical Leaders of the United States in front of 8,000 people. Higia’s team is invited to give a conference at the Harvard University and MIT Innovation Laboratories.

October

Higia collaborates with AXA Insurance to test EVA with 500 women.

Higia performs the launching event of EVA in Mexico City.

2018
January

Higia adds 8 new employees to the family and moves operations to Mexico City.

February

Higia is recognized as one of the most promising companies of 2018 by Forbes Magazine.

March

The Female Founders program is executed for the first time with 60 patients with the new version of EVA.

May

Higia Tech moves to Silicon Valley for YCombinator accelerator.

BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT TEAM

Julián Ríos

CEO & Co-Founder

julian.rios@higia.tech

After watching his mother’s painful battle against breast cancer, Julián Ríos Cantú made his personal mission to make an impact through early detection of breast cancer. Currently, he is the CEO and co-founder of Higia Technologies.

Julián has been awarded the National Medal for Technology and Innovation of Mexico and the Vanguard Award from Minerva University for his research about breast cancer detection technology. He has been a guest professor at the National Congress of Medicine of the United States and at the Harvard University and MIT Innovation Laboratories. In May 2017, Julián became the first Mexican to win the Global Student Entrepreneur Award. Recently, Higia has been appointed one of the 30 most promising companies of Mexico for 2018 by Forbes and EVA as one of the principal medical innovations by the National Congress of Medicine of the United States.
